- avoid old-fashioned language
- personalize your letter
- keep reader in mind
- use words that are simple and easy to understand
- use 'active voice'
- use neutral tone

- letterhead (optional)
- return address (writer)

    - a company

## PPT 7

memorandum - memo

- A business memo is a slightly short document used to transmit information within an organization.
- Memos are characterized by being brief, direct, and easy to navigate.
- They are less formal than letters but should maintain a professional style.

memo

- traditional
- simplified

**traditional memo**

To: (Audience – Individual or group)

From: (writer’s info – Name, Position)

Date: (Complete date should be written)

Subject: (Simple and direct)

**simplified memo**

5 June 2026 (Complete date should be written)

All staff (Audience – Individual or group)

Compulsory Training (Simple and direct)

**para**

number 2-4

general 2-3

*Introduction Paragraph*

main purpose + briefly explain

*Body Paragraph*

Explanation

*Concluding Paragraph*

General conclusion + Be direct

**Finally**

Keep memos brief and to the point
